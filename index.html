About Me

My name is Jacob Rodriguez, and I founded Unbounded Racing. From a lifelong passion for cars and performance driving. I was born on 09/05/2007, and ever since I was young, I’ve been deeply interested in vehicles — how they work, how they perform, and how they can be pushed to their limits.

Over the years, that interest turned into real hands-on experience. From learning about car modifications to working on vehicles and understanding performance builds, I’ve developed a strong connection to the automotive world. What started as a hobby quickly became something much bigger — a vision.

Velocity Drive Experience was created to give others the opportunity to experience what most people only dream about — driving high-performance, fully modified cars on real tracks in a safe and controlled environment. Not everyone has access to these types of vehicles, and this business is about making that experience possible.

My goal is to build more than just a business — I want to create a place where people can feel adrenaline, excitement, and passion for cars all in one experience. Whether it's your first time behind the wheel of a powerful car or you're already an enthusiast, this is a place built for you.

This is just the beginning, and I’m committed to growing Velocity Drive Experience into something unforgettable.
